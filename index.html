Introduction: What is Manhunt Kit?

Manhunt Kit is a PvP Legacy custom kit inspired by Dream's Manhunt challenge. With items like pearls, cobwebs, knockback swords, and lava buckets, it simulates the chaotic, chase-based gameplay seen in Dream's videos.
