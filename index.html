🔥 What is Axe PvP?

Axe PvP is a competitive combat style in Minecraft Java Edition 1.9+ that focuses on timing and raw damage over spam clicking. It prioritizes precision, shield breaks, and burst damage using axes instead of swords.

🪓 Advantages of Axes

  • Massive damage output — ideal for crit combos.
  • Instantly disables enemy shields for 5 seconds.
  • Breaks armor faster due to higher durability damage.
  • Pairs well with strength and critical hits.

🎯 PvP Mechanics & Tips

  • ⚡ Use cooldown timing — never spam swing.
  • 🛡️ Time shield breaks to open enemy defense.
  • 🌀 Strafe left-right to become harder to hit.
  • 🔮 Jump + hit mid-air for crits (time it perfectly).
  • 💨 Crouch before jump to reduce particle trails.

🛠️ Gear Recommendations

Armor: Netherite/Diamond with Protection IV
Axe: Netherite Axe, Sharpness V, Efficiency, Unbreaking
Extras: Shield, Strength Potions, Golden Apples, Pearls

⚙️ Combos & Openers

Start with a shield disable, then follow up with 2 timed crits. Use strafes to dodge hits and open up angles. If the enemy gaps, switch to pressuring with movement, then punish when they panic.
